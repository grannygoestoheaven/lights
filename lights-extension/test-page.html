Testing Instructions

  1. Install the Lights extension in your browser
  2. Hover over any word on this page (should highlight in bright cream white)
  3. Move your mouse vertically over words to test multi-word highlighting
  4. Click once on highlighted words to activate color cycling
  5. Click again to cycle through: Green → Blue → Red → Yellow → Purple → Green...
  6. Each color shows different information in the popup
  7. Press Escape to return to hover mode
  8. Try the extension popup to toggle settings

Expected Behavior:

  • Hover: Words turn bright cream white with subtle border
  • First Click: Words turn green, show definitions
  • Second Click: Words turn blue, show synonyms
  • Third Click: Words turn red, show stories/facts
  • Fourth Click: Words turn yellow, show etymology
  • Fifth Click: Words turn purple, show examples
  • Sixth Click: Cycle back to green
