Lights Extension

Enable word highlighting

Color Modes

Cream White - Hover (Default)
Green - Definitions
Blue - Synonyms
Red - Stories & Facts
Yellow - Etymology
Purple - Examples

How to use

  1. Hover over any word on a webpage (highlighted in cream white)
  2. Move mouse up/down to highlight more words
  3. Click once to activate and cycle through color modes
  4. Each color shows different information in the popup
  5. Press Escape to close and return to hover mode
